Dr.Quincy Brown

Dr.Quincy Brown is an American computer scientist and a former Senior Policy Advisor in the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy. She is a published academic and co-founder of blackcomputeHER.org, NationOfMakers.org and Black In Computing. She earned a master of science degree and was an assistant professor Bowie State University before she left to pursue careers in the government and nonprofit organizations. In 2020, Brown launched the organization blackcomputeHER.org with Jamika D. Burge and Jakita Thomas.The organization's mission is to support workforce development, computing education and technology education for Black women and girls through research, programs, and events.
